I've always loved whatever Eric Barbour over at [Metasonix][1] was up to, but this is something that I can really get behind. I've wondered in the past why someone with incredible vacuum tube skills had never made an amp that was completely hybrid and looked as grotesque as this does.

Ladies and gentlemen, I give you the [Metasonix G-1000 Guitar Amplifier][2]. I can only imagine how cool this sounds.

If you have never checked out Metasonix, head over there, and indulge. Also, if you know of any other cool makers of crazy stuff like this, send them to me!
