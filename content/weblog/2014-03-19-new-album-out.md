<span class="largestartfont">M</span>y newest release, oldowan, has been released on [Functional Equivalent Recordings][1]. You can buy a limited edition cassette (48 of them made), which is an imprinted cassette within a black muscletone custom case and comes inside of a drawstring burlap bag.

_Tate Eskew explores minimalist guitar drone in two 11 minute pieces with the release of "oldowan". Exploration of regenerative ecology and indigenous culture inspired the two compositions. Instances of calm reflectiveness are slowly interrupted by passages of anxiety and moments of contentment._
