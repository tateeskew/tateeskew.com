It's been quite some time since I have updated here. There's been a lot going on and I've been trying to stay focused on the projects in the hopper.  
Over the last 10 months or so, here are some things that have been going on.

I recorded a 10&#8243; record with Nashville natives, [Ttotals][1]. It is called "Silver on Black" and you can take a listen and buy it here:

<center>  
</center>

We also recorded two new songs for a 7&#8243; series that will be put out by [Sonic Cathedral][2]. One of the songs will be selected by the label and then mastered by Sonic Boom from Spacemen 3 for release.

I've also started recording an album with [Deli][3]. I believe we have 4-5 songs tracked and continue work as time is available. I think it's shaping up to be a wonderful record. It will be released on [Functional Equivalent Recordings][4] sometime in the future.

Over the last 11 months I have been recording my newest solo record, as well. At this point, I have no idea when it will be finished, but it's very close. Andy Gregg and [Josh Fuson][5] are sharing percussion duties and [Nate Dort][6] has contributed bass to a few songs. Hopefully, I will have something to share soon.

Finally, I also have an album worth of ambient guitar music for a new release. It will see the light of day at some point as well.

If you are interested in hanging out at our [farm][7] and recording some music, practicing some permaculture, or brewing some beer, just let me know!
