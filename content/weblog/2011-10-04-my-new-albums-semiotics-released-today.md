This has been a long time coming, but as of today my new album Semiotics comes out today on [Function Equivalent Recordings][1]. I've spent an immeasurable amount of time on this recording. It has been recorded in my home studio over the past few years as I have moved around. At times, I had no studio and I wasn't able to work on the album at all. Other times, I was so full of ideas I couldn't get them down quick enough. This is the object of that process. 

You can buy the record here:  [Buy Tate Eskew's Semiotics Record][2]
