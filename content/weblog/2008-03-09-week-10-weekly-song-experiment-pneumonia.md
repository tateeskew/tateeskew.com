I'm not sure how I did it this week. I started recording this song at 9 o'clock tonight. It's now 10:21. I recorded this up in the bonus room bathroom. I've been sick as all get out this past week and I'm still not over it. I couldn't sing and I feel pretty bad at the moment. We also are living amongst boxes at the new house. This week was a struggle.

This week's song is entitled P_neumonia_. It's how I feel and maybe I should go to the doc to find out. Anyway, head over to the [weekly song experiment page][1] to give it a listen.
