Well, the summer heat has put its filthy hands on Middle Tennessee. the good part is that the garden loves it and fresh strawberries were picked just last week. Incredible…

Believe it or not, I've started writing new songs when time is available and I'm very happy with what has come about so far. I miss very much having my studio, as it's a huge part of my writing process, but I'm also excited to see how the new stuff turns out since I don't have that at my disposal for now. With that said, I'm going to try to finish my album _Semiotics_ by year-end (before baby #2 comes). If it's not finished by then, it may be a while…

Thanks for the emails, everyone. I still find it hard to believe that people read this crap.
