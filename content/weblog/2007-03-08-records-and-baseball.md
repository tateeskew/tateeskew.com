It's a good time of the year. Things are incredibly busy here as of late. I just finished tracking basics for the new [Sequoia][1] album here at Zero Art Studio and spirits are high there. I've also found a little bit of time to work on _Semiotics_, which is taking much longer to do because I keep adding more and more projects to the plate.

[Mock Orange][2] was recently here at Zero Art Studio mixing their upcoming album, which is still untitled at this point I believe. It sounds good, trust me.

I also have recently completed a live Zero Art Radio session with [Patrick Krief][3] from [The Dears][4]. Patrick has just released a solo album that is really incredible.

That's it for now.
