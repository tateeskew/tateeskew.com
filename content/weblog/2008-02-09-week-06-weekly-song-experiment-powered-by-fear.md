This week I was able to sing again. The head cold has passed. I was able to knock this song out within a few days this week. We've been incredibly busy working on the new house so that we can get moved in, but I'm enjoying trying to cram music time in. It really does feel great to just create it and let it go. I don't know how many people are out there listening, but it really doesn't matter. It's out there.

To those that are listening, this week's song is called P_owered by Fear_. Head over to the [weekly song experiment][1] page and put it to your ears.
