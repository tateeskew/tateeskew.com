A few years ago I had the pleasure of sitting in my studio with my friend, [Patrick Krief][1], and recording the 4 songs below. He was in town performing with his band, [The Dears][2], and we were able to get some time before the show to knock these recordings out. We recorded live to 2-track using a single microphone and ate some brownies after the session. Patrick is a passionate musician and I think it really comes through in these performances.

I plan on putting more of these [Zero Art Sessions][3] up as I record them or dig up old stuff. Once the new studio is up, I plan on making this live session experiment a semi-permanent fixture here on my site. Enjoy the music.
